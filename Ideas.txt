Theme is A Small World

Ideas:
On a small planet and have to shoot around the back to hit other players/yourself
Teleport around the place - makes it a small world
Exploring a small planet --> on a ship above the clouds. Go down to explore and find objects

Actual Game Idea:
Explore a small planet from your dropship. You move around a grid based game and send down mining/exploration/archaelogy bots.
Elements:
Iron, Gold, Platinum, Carbon, Oxygen, Water, Food and Helium-3.
Find 4 artifacts to win.
